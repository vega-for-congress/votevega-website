We are looking for high school and college students who want real campaign experience on an independent run for Congress. This is not a casual volunteer role. It is a working internship with responsibility, structure, and direct involvement in the campaign.

Apply Now See Campaign Events

Internship Snapshot

  • Runs after the Fourth of July through around the middle of August.
  • Commitment of 4 to 5 days a week working on the campaign.
  • Core hours are 10 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Thursday.
  • Saturdays are for special campaign events and field activity.
  • Stipend provided for transportation plus a little extra to cover costs.

What You Get

Real Campaign Experience

Work inside an independent campaign and see how political organizing, outreach, events, and field operations actually function day to day.

Professional Support

Strong interns can earn letters of recommendation, referrals, and practical experience that helps with future jobs, school applications, and organizing work.

Expectations

We need applicants who can show up consistently, communicate clearly, and treat this as a real summer commitment.

Schedule And Timing

The internship starts after the Fourth of July and runs until around the middle of August, with hours from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM Monday through Thursday and Saturdays reserved for campaign events and special actions.
